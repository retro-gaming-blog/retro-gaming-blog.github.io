Unboxing the Past: Top 10 Retro Gaming Handhelds You Would Love to Own Again

Oh, the good old days! Remember all those hours spent ignoring parents, teachers, and the great outdoors while your thumbs danced on your handheld console? As an adult, you may have a smartphone that does anything and everything, but does it truly spark the same joy as those simple, blocky pixels did? Well, if you'd like to journey back in time, dust off your denim jacket, crank up the Nirvana, and join me as we dive into the top 10 retro gaming handhelds you would absolutely love to own again!

1. Nintendo Game Boy

Let's kick things off with the granddaddy of them all, the Nintendo Game Boy. This bad boy had a bit of a green screen, didn't it? But who cared, we loved it! I mean, Tetris on the go? Yes, please! If you were a Game Boy owner, you were basically the coolest kid on the block.

2. Sega Game Gear

Alright, now we're entering Sega territory. Does anyone remember the Game Gear? This device was literally ahead of its time with a full-color backlit screen - it was like staring into the future! Pair that with Sonic the Hedgehog and you've got yourself a match made in handheld heaven.

3. Atari Lynx

Who here had the Atari Lynx? Anyone? No? Well, you missed out! This monster machine was the first handheld with a color LCD. Yeah, it was as big as a house brick and ate batteries like a beast, but with games like Blue Lightning and California Games, it was worth every triple-A.

4. Game Boy Color

Okay, let's get back to Nintendo. Welcome to the Game Boy Color! This little gem added a much-needed splash of color to our handheld gaming. Plus, it brought us the legendary Pokémon Gold and Silver. What, were you more into Crystal? Well, to each their own!

5. Neo Geo Pocket Color

Next on the list, we have the Neo Geo Pocket Color. Now this was a niche device, wasn't it? If you had one of these, you were truly a hardcore handheld enthusiast. Or just really into SNK vs. Capcom.

6. Nintendo DS

Alright, who didn't have a Nintendo DS? It’s just not possible! With its clamshell design and two screens, one of them being touch sensitive, this was the epitome of cool. It was like having a mini laptop with Mario Kart in it!

7. PlayStation Portable (PSP)

Sony wasn't going to sit on the sidelines while Nintendo had all the fun. Enter the PlayStation Portable or PSP. This sleek device wasn't just for games, you could watch movies and listen to music too. It was like the Swiss Army knife of handhelds!

8. Game Boy Advance SP

Now, we can't forget the Game Boy Advance SP. This little marvel had a rechargeable battery, a backlight, and a compact clamshell design. It was basically the Ferrari of Game Boys.

9. Sega Nomad

A portable Sega Genesis? Yes, please! The Sega Nomad was like carrying an arcade in your pocket. Well, if your pocket was the size of a small backpack. Sure, it was bulky and power-hungry, but it could play Genesis cartridges. Genesis cartridges people!

10. TurboExpress

Rounding up the list we've got the TurboExpress, a handheld console that could play TurboGrafx-16 games. It also had a TV tuner so you could catch your favorite shows. Who needed cable when you had this beast?!

So there you have it, folks. A nostalgic journey through the greatest gadgets of our childhoods. Makes you want to dig out your old consoles, doesn't it? Or maybe hunt some down on eBay? Whichever route you go, remember, it's all about the joy of gaming. Even if the screen is a bit green!
