Top 10 Retro Gaming Handhelds: Unveiling the Classics You Can't Miss in 2023

Hey there, gaming enthusiasts! Let me guess, you've been longing for that sweet, sweet nostalgia, haven't you? Well, buckle up because we're about to hop in our time machine and take a trip down memory lane. I've compiled a list of the top 10 retro gaming handhelds that you simply can't miss in 2023. Ready to hit the pixelated road? Of course, you are!

1. Game Boy

Ah, Game Boy. The handheld that needs no introduction, but I'll give it one anyway because it's just that awesome. This grey brick of wonder was the first to show us that we could take our gaming on the go. Tetris, Pokémon, Super Mario Land, anyone?

2. Game Boy Color

What's better than a Game Boy? A Game Boy with color! Suddenly, you could catch your Pokémon in full technicolor glory. It was revolutionary, mind-blowing, and oh-so-colorful. It's like Dorothy stepping into Oz, but with fewer flying monkeys.

3. Game Gear

Remember when Sega was still in the console game? The Game Gear was their answer to the Game Boy. Backlit screen, color display, and a way to drain 6 AA batteries in what felt like minutes. It was like carrying around your own little arcade machine, if only your pockets were big enough.

4. Neo Geo Pocket Color

You've probably heard of Neo Geo, right? Well, they had a handheld too! The Neo Geo Pocket Color was the underdog that could duke it out with the best of 'em. With a clicky thumbstick and some awesome games, it was like having a tiny arcade in your hands.

5. Atari Lynx

Atari Lynx, remember that one? This beast was the first handheld with color and a backlit screen. Was it a Game Boy killer? Not exactly, but it was like a disco party in your hands.

6. Nintendo DS

If one screen is good, two must be better, right? That was Nintendo's gamble with the DS, and boy, did it pay off! Whether you were solving puzzles in Brain Age or catching monsters in Pokémon, the DS had something for everyone.

7. PSP

Sony's first foray into handhelds was a sight to behold. It was sleek, it was sexy, and it played games that looked almost as good as your PS2. Not to mention, it played movies! Who didn't want to watch Spider-Man 2 on a tiny screen?

8. Game Boy Advance

Ah, the Game Boy Advance. The handheld that took the world by storm and gave us some of the best portable games of all time. Whether you were smashing brothers in Super Smash Bros or exploring the Hoenn region in Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire, the GBA had you covered.

9. Sega Nomad

Ever wanted to take your Genesis games on the go? Well, the Sega Nomad let you do just that. It was like carrying around a portable 90s arcade. Sure, it ate batteries like Pac-Man eats dots, but it was worth it.

10. WonderSwan

This quirky little handheld from Japan had some of the most unique and innovative games around. Whether it was the puzzle-solving of Gunpey or the RPG goodness of Final Fantasy, the WonderSwan was a gem waiting to be discovered.

So, there you have it! The top 10 retro handhelds you can't miss in 2023! Now, I have to ask, are you ready to get your game on and relive some of the best moments from the golden age of handheld gaming? Of course, you are! Game on, my friends. Game on!
